About me

In the midst of 2020, sandwiched between a Zoom graduation in psychology and biology and doom-scrolling TikTok, a dormant passion for programming resurfaced. Memories of my teenage years, meticulously creating templates for my Tumblr account using HTML and CSS, flooded back. Each line of code I write and every challenge I tackle stands as proof of my relentless dedication and enthusiasm for programming.

MY PROJECTS

SpaceBnb

Spacebnb is a platform where homeowners meet travelers in search of comfortable and budget-friendly accommodations. Drawing inspiration from Airbnb, it offers a comprehensive online space for residential stays, vacation spots, and unique travel experiences. Users also enjoy full capabilities to create, read, update, and delete their reviews and listings.

GlobalGrub

GlobalGrub is a food delivery platform where users can seamlessly order from a variety of local restaurants. Drawing inspiration from Uber Eats, it provides a comprehensive online marketplace for diverse cuisines and culinary delights. Users have complete control to create, read, update, and delete their orders and reviews. Additionally, they can effortlessly filter and search for their favorite dining spots .

Live Link Github Repo

Clockdn

Clockdn is a professional networking platform where users can foster career connections and share insights. Drawing inspiration from LinkedIn, it offers a dynamic space for users to create, read, update, and delete their posts and comments. Beyond that, members can also 'like' content and establish meaningful connections with fellow users to expand their professional network.
